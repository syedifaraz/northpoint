Cold Chain

In Pharmaceutical Industry , temprature-controlled drug products otherwise known as “COLD CHAIN” are increasingly finding their way into patient therapeutic regimens. These products are largely prevalent in the specialty space, particularly due to distinct nuances with refrigerated or room temperature handling. Also now a days Vaccination is an effective public health measure to prevent and control a number of infectious diseases.

However, since vaccines are biological products and are sensitive to both heat and cold, they need to be maintained within a narrow range of temperatures. This range, which is between +2°C and +8°C with a target +5°C, does not allow for refreezing or storage at room temperature. Thus, having a Cold Chain is one of the most important requirement for a big pharmaceutical distributors to maintain the efficaciy of the products which requires these special conditions.

All your concerns regarding cold cahin will be taken care of as North Point being one of the leading pharmaceutical distributor of the valley is in the posession of 2000 litres cold room space for cold chain products at its premesis.
