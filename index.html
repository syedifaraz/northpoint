About Us

Northpoint is a well-established leading pharmaceutical distribution firm with stockist ship of more than twenty-five pharmaceutical companies and super-stockist for four companies based in Kashmir Valley.

Engaged in the process of distribution of medicine, surgical products and diagnostic tools, North Point is playing a vital role within the pharmaceutical industry of Kashmir valley by working with clients from both governments as well private sectors.

North Point is located in commercial hub of Srinagar,having an office/storage space measuring than more than 12,000 square feet with the cold storage facility of around 2000 litres feet.

North Point is also actively catering to the government hospitals in the Valley playing vital role in supporting them for productive functionality.

Mile Stones

  • North Point started by name and style of "Syed Trading Company" in the year 1995 from the heart of Srinagar (J&K) city in the Polo View complex located in Lal Chowk.

  • Initially, the company focused on the distribution of champion photochemistry, x-ray films and kiran x-rays and in year 1996 became franchise partners with JK Pharma. Within the next few years North Point was franchise partners with other major houses like MAY & BAKER , Merind ETC.

  • In the year 1998,the franchise business operations were converted into stockist-ship, and gradually became partners with other ethical pharmaceutical houses as stockists to provoide better service to all our clients.

  • After five working-years the head office was shifted to Shaheed Gung, Srinagar J&K 190010 ,behind the old secretariat. By then, North Point had become stockist for seven companies and super-stockist for one company.

  • In 2005 the business was relocated to the heart of the pharmaceutical hub of the city, Karanager. Thus, located at the most reachable place North Point became one of the largest distributors of pharmaceuticals in whole Kashmir Valley.

  • In 2008 North Point was the stockist for more than ten companies and super-stockist for two companies.

  • In 2012 North Point bacame one of the prominent stockist for most of the MNC hubs like Jhonson and Jhonson Pvt. Ltd etc. and the largest stockist of the Kashmir valley.
