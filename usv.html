USV PVT LTD

USV Private Limited (formerly USV Limited) is an Indian multinational pharmaceutical and biotechnology company based in Mumbai, India. The company was founded by Vithal Balkrishna Gandhi and Leena Tewari, his granddaughter, is the chairperson. The company is focused on the development of small-molecule active pharmaceutical ingredients along with custom research services for drug development.
