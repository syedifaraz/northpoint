Super-Stockist Distribution

North Point work as Super-Stockist for four major pharmaceutical companies of India.

  • WOCKHARDT LTD.

  • USV PVT LTD.

  • CENTAUR PHARMACEUTICALS PVT LTD.

  • UNIZA HEALTHCARE LLP

In super-stockists Operations companies authorised stockists place orders directly to North Point which is working as the super-stockist for the concerned company. North Point thus places purchase order as per stockists demand directly to the company which inturn bills the ordered products. Business Process operations for North Point As Super-stockist is as follows:

  • Customer places orders to chemists and recieves the goods as per prescription.
  • Chemists places orders to stockists or sub-stockists and recieves the goods from either stockists or sub-stockists.
  • Stockists places orders to Super-Stockists (North Point) and recieves the goods as per order.

  • Super-Stockists places orders to Company and inturn recieves the goods as per order.

Institutional Supplies Distribution

In instutional supplies distribution business model the companies associated with the particular government or private institution (hospitals, nursing homes etc.) authorise North Point as their authorised distributors/ suppliers for that institution. North Point places orders to the company on behalf the the institution for which it is authorised which in turn bills the requirement. North Point then bills the required goods to the concerned institution.

Business Process operations for North Point As Institutional Supplier Distributor is as follows:

  • Concerned Intitution (Hospital, Nursing Hme etc.) places orders to authorised distributor (North Point) for the company it is associated with.
  • Authorised distributor (North Point) places the recieved order to Company as per requirement.
  • Company Bills the goods as per requirement to Authorised distributor (North Point) which inturn bills the recieved goods to the concerned institution .

North Point has been associated with Kashmir Valley's leading Government Hospital Sher-e-Kashmir Institute of Medical Sciences (SKIMS) for more than 5 years as its institutional supplier.

Institutional Supplies Services

In the instititional supplies service model companies associated with the particular government or private institution (hospitals, nursing homes etc.) authorise North Point as their authorised service provoiders for that institution. Concerned institution places orders directly to the company which in turn bills the requirement. North Point facilitates the supplies by provoiding logistics and other services on behalf of the company.

Business Process operations for North Point As Institutional Supplier Service Provoider is as follows:

  • Concerned Intitution (Hospital, Nursing Hme etc.) raise requirements for the company it is associated with.
  • Service provoider (North Point) forwords the recieved requirement to Company.
  • Company Bills the goods as per requirement to the cocerned institution and sends the goods to Authorised service provoider (North Point).
  • Service provoider (North Point) delivers the goods to the concerned institution and facilitate in release payment and other supply processes required by the company .

North Point is associated with Jhonson and Jhonson Pvt.Ltd. as service provoider for Kashmir Valley's leading Government Hospital Sher-e-Kashmir Institute of Medical Sciences (SKIMS)
